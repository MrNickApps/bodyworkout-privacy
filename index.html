Privacy Policy – Body Workout

Last updated: July 2026

Body Workout ("the app") is developed by MrNick Apps. This privacy policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data We Collect

The app collects the following data:

  • Workout data you enter manually (plans, workouts, exercises, sets, reps, weights) and their dates
  • Email address — only if you choose to create an account to sync your data across devices. Account creation is optional; the app works fully without it.
  • Anonymous device identifier — assigned automatically for data storage

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. Authentication

By default, the app uses anonymous authentication via Firebase — no login required. Optionally, you can create an account with an email address and password to sync your workouts across devices. Your password is handled securely by Firebase Authentication and is never stored by us directly.

3. Data Storage

Your workout data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore, associated with your user ID. Data is stored in the European Union (europe-west1) and encrypted in transit.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to any third parties, except as required by the infrastructure services listed below.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore) – Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (in-app purchase management) – Privacy Policy

6. Deleting Your Data

You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time:

Delete individual workouts, plans, or exercises

Long-press any entry within the app to delete it.

Delete your account and all associated data

To permanently delete your account and all data associated with it:

  1. Send an email to mrnickapps@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete My Account".
  3. We will permanently delete your account and all associated workout data within 30 days.

What is deleted: your account, email address, all workout data (plans, workouts, exercises, history), and all settings. What is kept: nothing — all data is fully removed. Anonymous purchase records may be retained by Google Play as required for financial/legal compliance.

7. Children's Privacy

Body Workout is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
