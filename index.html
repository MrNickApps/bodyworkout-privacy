Body Workout - Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Body Workout ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy describes what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Collect

The app stores the following data to provide its functionality:

  • Workout data – plans, workouts, exercises, sets, reps, weights, and workout history you enter in the app.
  • App preferences – theme, units, and settings.
  • Anonymous user ID – automatically generated by Firebase Authentication to associate your data with your device.
  • Email address (optional) – only if you choose to link an email to your account for cross-device sync or account recovery.
  • Purchase records – handled by RevenueCat and the app store (Google Play / Apple App Store) if you purchase Pro.

We do not collect your name, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore, associated only with your anonymous user ID. Data is stored in the European Union (europe-west1).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to any third parties, except as required by the infrastructure services listed below.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore) – Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (in-app purchase management) – Privacy Policy

6. Account & Data Deletion

You can delete individual workouts, plans, or exercises at any time within the app.

To request complete deletion of your account and all associated data, email mrnickapps@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account". Your data will be removed from Firebase within 30 days.

What is deleted: all workout data, plans, exercises, history, preferences, and your anonymous user ID.
What is kept: nothing — all data is fully removed. Anonymous purchase records may be retained by Google Play or Apple as required for financial/legal compliance.

7. Children's Privacy

Body Workout is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
